Getting There

Access gps instructions to safely get to your destination. Want to get there quick? Take a slow scenic route? We'll show the ways to get there, and you pick the one that works for you. Don't have a car? Choose from a variety of car-free and public transit friendly destinations. Spend less time trying to find out how to get there, and more time finding the right snacks to bring along.

Listen

No good road trip is without good music. Choose from a variety of curated playlists tailored for each trip, or upload your own personal favourites. Life is a highway, and while we don't always suggest riding it all night long, we do suggest a good set of tunes to get you where you're going.

Weather

Get up to date weather forecasts for your destination to help you plan your daily activities. Save an itinerary, and easily make changes to suit your needs. Not into a muddy walk down a forest trail? Check out some local breweries, museums and other cool indoor establishments.

Notes

Easily make notes throughout your trip with our online journalling platform. You can choose to upload your own photos and share your journey, or browse travel notes from others. Come across a cool hiking trail? Record store? A neat looking tree? Let others know!
